A little bit about me

I started my journey with tech at a young age by taking a computer science class in high school. I had only wanted to learn more about computers, but to my surprise the class was actually a programming class! I fell in love with coding and found it came to me quite easily. I dove in head first by finishing classes at a local junior college and landing a job working on a mainframe before my 20th birthday.

In a move I will never regret, I quit my job and chose to follow my husband, who is enlisted with the Air Force. Needless to say, my five year career plan did not work out!

We lived in Tokyo, Japan for three wonderful years before moving to our favorite oceanside oasis, Rota, Spain. While overseas, we took time to travel and to grow our family. Seven years, nine countries, and three children later, here we are! While these past few years have been simply amazing, I am eager to get back into coding once my husband ends his military career in spring 2017.

I have been studying and practicing full-stack web development ever since we decided to move back home. I cannot wait to be able to put my new skills to good use for you!
